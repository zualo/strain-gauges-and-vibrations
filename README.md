A collection of the non-MATLAB labs and homeworks created for the ME 646 course at the University of New Hampshire.

After a while of writing these in MATLAB, I began to miss some of the features provided by other high-level languages I've worked with in the past and am working with in the present. I've recently been introduced to the Julia programming language by professor Marek Petrik here at the University of New Hampshire and have greatly enjoyed writing assignments in it. Python is nice too, but to any peers that may stumble upon this: try Julia!

Homeworks are stored in folders with the $n$H notation and labs are stored in folders with the $n$L notation.
